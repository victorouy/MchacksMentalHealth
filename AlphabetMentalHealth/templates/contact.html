Get support

You are not alone and your well-being is important.

If you are in immediate danger, please call 911
or go to your local emergency department.

If you are in crisis or someone you know has suicidal thoughts or
behaviours: Canada Suicide Prevention Service

  1-833-456-4566 (24/7)
  text 45645 (4 pm to 12 am ET)
To get help and mental support, contact any of the resources below.
To gain information about mental health and therapy, visit
Canadian Mental Health Association to find your local CMHA.
  https://cmha.ca/find-your-cmha
Association des psychothérapeutes psychanalytiques du Québec
  http://appq.com/
Ordre des psychologues du Québec

Helplines

If you just want to talk with someone about current issues
(loneliness, stress, relationships), contact Tel-Aide for
anonymous and confidential support.
  514-935-1101 (27/4)
If you are aged 5 to 29 and need assistance from professional
counsellors, contact Kids help phone:
  1-800-668-6868 (toll-free)
  text 686868
  https://kidshelpphone.ca/
Help for Indigenous peoples: Hope for Wellness
  1-855-242-3310 (toll-free)
  https://www.hopeforwellness.ca/
